yavis

This repo consists of many strange scripts, based on bash, and unix tools such as 'curl', 'awk' and 'grep'.

11mh.net

A sign-in robot for 11mh.net.

8.com

A data crawler for MyGirl and some sort of that.

cnki

A papers downloader for CNKI, when at ISS.

ipgw

PKU gateway connector, just like IPGW but within command line.

iss

ISS gatewat connector, when at ISS.

kali.tv

A video sniffer for KALI.TV; all is free ! :-)

keeptunnel

A auto connector for ssh proxy.

id

Identity No. crawler, from shenfenzhenghaodaquan

livecounter

A counter for webcast, i.e. DOUYU and Huya.

Just nothing.
